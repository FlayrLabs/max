What it does

One keystroke from anything
you'd ever ask a computer.

⌨︎

Always a keystroke away

A floating Liquid Glass command bar appears on your shortcut, anywhere. Type what you want, watch Max work, then close it — your conversation picks up where you left off.

↻

Loops

Schedule Max. "Every morning, summarize my calendar." It runs on its own and pings you when it matters.

⎚

It sees your screen

Ask about whatever you're looking at. Max reads the screen — or just the text, privately — and answers in place.

⌥

Full hands-on control

Shell, AppleScript, files, apps. Max doesn't describe what to do — it does it, then tells you what happened.

◍

Your key, your model

Claude, OpenAI, or a fully-local model via Ollama. Keys live encrypted in the Keychain and never touch a server.

⌘

Many Macs

Connect your other Macs over SSH. Max is aware of all of them and can act on any one from a single conversation.
